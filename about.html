About Me:

I originally became interested in the Tech industry when I started my Certificate III of IT at TasTAFE in Tasmania.

While studying the certificate, I was particularly interested in the programming clusters of the course.

I learned how to use 'HTML' and 'CSS' to create webpages, and I also made an application lodging portal app using 'Python'.

I continued self-studying using resources like 'FreeCodeCamp' and 'The Odin Project' to further my coding skills. I have become quite proficient in Javascript by doing so, and I have uploaded all of my projects to GitHub. To see some of my main projects, please click on the portfolio tab.

I love using the skills I've learned to create interactive websites, and I really enjoy the design element of front end development in particular.
